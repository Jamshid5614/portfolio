CASE STUDY

PMR — online platform & responsive website design

MY ROLE

Creative Direction
UX / UI
Icon Design
Photography

CLIENT

PMR

YEAR

2016 — 2018

With over 20 years of experience, PMR serves more than 500 global clients offering their market research and expert analysis products. I was asked to re-create their platform from scratch, and improve the visual appeal of their website and all their products, while making them informative and easy to use, in order to reflect their vision and leading position on the market.

Alongside Krzysztof Rusnarczyk, who was responsible for the front-end development, I worked as PMR’s long-term creative partner and UX / UI / Web design specialist over the course of almost two years.

I established a new visual language, an extensive library of user interface elements, and designed over 40 individual pages, working closely with the client, through a collaborative review process. The main goal was to improve the user experience to make it fresh, intuitive and accessible across all platforms, which elevated the company’s online presence and their products.
