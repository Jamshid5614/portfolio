CASE STUDY

Sonicsmith — responsive website design

MY ROLE

Creative Direction
UX / UI
Icon Design
Photography

CLIENT

Sonicsmith

YEAR

2016

Established in 2016 by an American electronics engineer and an Israeli music producer, Sonicsmith is a Krakow based company that designs and manufactures professional audio equipment. I was commissioned to create an engaging design for their new website, highlighting their products and information about the company.

I teamed up with Krzysztof Rusnarczyk, who was responsible for the front-end and back-end development. Our goal was to create a fresh, simple and engaging online experience.

The new design language was built around consistent and bold typography, a generous use of whitespace, and a green accent color representing the surface of an electronic printed circuit board (the color is also used on Sonicsmith’s products), which gave the company a very distinctive look and helped to stand out in the market.
