Photography

YEAR

2013 — 2019

My favorite way to take a break from the screen is to get out on my motorbike, preferably for days at a time, with my camera in tow. You can check out the photos and read about these trips on my blog. And you can visit my Instagram account.

Below you can see a selection of photos from some of my travels. Most of them were shot with a Canon 550D and an EF-S 17-85 mm f/4-5.6 IS USM lens. In 2017 I upgraded to a Canon 80D and a better lens, which offers a significantly better picture quality.

Landscapes

Photos taken in Faroe Islands, Austria, Croatia, Egypt, Germany, Greece, Italy, Poland, Romania, Slovenia and Switzerland mostly during my motorcycle trips around those countries (excluding Egypt and Faroes).
